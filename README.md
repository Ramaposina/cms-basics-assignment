# Salesforce Summer Program

**1. What is CRM?**

CRM stands for **Customer Relationship Management**.

It is a technology used to manage customer relationships and interactions.

### CRM Helps Organizations To:

- Collaborate internally and externally
- Track business metrics
- Communicate through multiple channels
- Gather customer insights

---

## 2. Why Companies Use Salesforce

- Stores customer information centrally
- Tracks sales opportunities
- Improves communication
- Automates processes
- Provides reports and analytics
- Supports customer service management

**3. Accounts, Contacts, and Opportunities**

**Account**
An Account represents a company or organization you do business with.

**Contact**
A Contact is a person who works at ana account or company

**Opporutunity**
An Opporutunity represents a potential sales deal or business transaction.


**4. Real-World Mapping**

| CRM Object | Meaning |
|------------|---------|

| Contact    | Stores customer details |
